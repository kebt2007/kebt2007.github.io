What are illusions?

According to Google, the definiton of illusion is "a thing that is or is likely to be wrongly perceived or interpreted by the senses." Like in the photo to the left, the photo appears to be 3D and also moving. Our museum/library is dedicated to art and exhibits taking visitors throught the magical world of illusions.

Dual-Buildings

As assigned, our dual-building approach plans to incorporate both aspects of a library and an illusion-based museum. On one side, you can concentrate on yourself in a quiet space and work on whatever you desire. On the other, you can concentrate on all the fascinating sights of illusions and what we have to portray to public eyes. The two buildings joining as one depicts the true gift of public outings and tourist attractions of the sort.

Amazing Exhibits

Our artists have created some beautiful pieces that are worth feasting your eyes on. Thinking outside the box, we found ones and created illusions inspired by other artists that makes you feel involved, give different messages, and betters the local community.

Places to Chillax

In the library, you have a space to concentrate on yourself, whether that involves reading, writing, typing, and more. We have plenty bookshelves of imported books that can either help you study or intrigue you to find a new series. Across the vestibule, we have a lobby that allows occupants to chill. Across the bridge, we have an outdoor lounge to enjoy the view of the area. On the third floor, we have more lounges, study rooms, and conference rooms to discuss among others on a temperate basis. You can also have a bit of fun in the arts and crafts, 3D printing, and the multitude of computer rooms that access the Internet. All these rooms will have comfortable seating and other common amenities.
